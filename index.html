About The Event

A hackathon is a collaborative event where programmers, developers, and designers come together to create innovative solutions or projects within a limited time frame, typically 24-72 hours. It fosters creativity, teamwork, and hands-on coding experience.

Event Schedule

Time Activity Details
10:00 AM Opening Ceremony Keynote from Industry Expert, Overview of Hackathon Theme, Rules, and Criteria
11:30 AM Problem Statement Announcement & Team Formation Problem statements or challenges are revealed; participants form teams or join pre-registered ones
1:00 PM Lunch Break Networking over lunch with tech professionals
2:30 PM Hackathon Coding Begins Teams start coding and developing their solutions
4:00 PM Final Submission & Demos Teams submit their final projects and showcase their solutions through live demos
11:00 PM Judging & Award Ceremony Judges evaluate submissions, and winners are announced based on creativity, technical complexity, and impact

Hackathon Rules

1.Eligibility: Participants must be of legal age (18+), and teams are usually 2-5 members.
2.Team Formation: Teams can form before or during the hackathon; one person per team.
3.Project Development: All work must be done during the event using publicly available tools.
4.Submission: Projects must be submitted by the deadline with all required materials.
5.Ownership: Teams own their projects and intellectual property.
